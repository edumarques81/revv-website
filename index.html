How it works

From zero to coached in under 30 seconds.

Step 01

Open the app

No onboarding questionnaire. Revv opens straight to Today's Workout — AI-suggested based on your history or a coach's program.

Step 02

Follow or build

Accept the AI's plan or jump into the workout builder. Search exercises, set your reps and weight, and get going. It's your session.

Step 03

Track every rep

Log sets in real time. Connect to cardio machines for live data. The app keeps the pace — you just have to show up and lift.

Step 04

AI adapts to you

After every session, the AI updates your profile. Next time it opens, the plan is already smarter. No manual tweaks needed — it just learns.
