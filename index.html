How it works

Three steps to your first coached session.

01

Open Revv, scan for nearby FTMS devices, tap to connect. No cables, no setup — just Bluetooth.

02

Choose from built-in interval programs or load one your coach prescribed. Set your target zones and go.

Revv listens to every pedal stroke. When you drift off target, your coach speaks up — and backs off when you're dialled in.
